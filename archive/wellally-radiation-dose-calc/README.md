# WellAll Radiation Dose Calculator

[![中文](https://img.shields.io/badge/Language-中文-red)](README.zh.md)

L4 computation model for calculating and tracking cumulative CT radiation dose. Non-diagnostic.

## Capabilities
- Dose calculation from modality-provided DLP.
- Accumulated dose tracking per patient.
- Safety threshold alerts and reporting.
- Trend analysis over time.

## Inputs & Basis
- DLP-to-effective-dose conversion.
- Body-part weighting factors.
- Age adjustments.
- References to international dose guidelines.

## Use Cases
- Imaging centers tracking patient cumulative dose across visits.
- Quality and safety teams monitoring threshold exceedance.
- Patient-facing dose passport to increase transparency.

## Disclaimer
Computes dose values only; does not assess medical risk or replace clinical judgment.
